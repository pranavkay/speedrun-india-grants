# BIRAC Product Commercialization Program

## Overview
Supports Indian biotech startups in scaling validated technologies to commercial readiness. Funding covers production scale-up, pilot manufacturing, validation studies, and regulatory preparation for **up to 24 months**.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Early to growth (validated technology)
- **Sectors**: Biotechnology, healthcare
- **Entity Type**: Indian biotech startups
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Type | Grant for commercialization |
| Duration | Up to 24 months |
| Covers | Production scale-up, pilot mfg, validation, regulatory |
| Equity | None |
