# MeitY AI Innovation Programme

## Overview
Grants up to **₹10 crore** with access to national AI infrastructure including computing resources and datasets. Targets AI projects addressing Indian societal challenges. Complementary to the broader [[IndiaAI Mission]] ecosystem.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to early (working AI product/prototype)
- **Sectors**: AI/ML applications for societal challenges
- **Entity Type**: Indian startups, research orgs
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Amount | Up to ₹10 Crore |
| Type | Grant |
| Equity | None |
| Additional | Access to AI compute infra, datasets |
