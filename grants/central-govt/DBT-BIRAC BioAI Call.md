# DBT-BIRAC BioAI Call

## Overview
Joint call for proposals on **Bio-AI for High-Performance Biomanufacturing**, establishing hubs under the BioE3 Policy. At the intersection of AI and biotechnology, targeting startups building AI-driven biomanufacturing solutions.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to early
- **Sectors**: AI + Biotechnology, biomanufacturing
- **Entity Type**: Startups, research orgs, industry
- **Geography**: Pan-India
