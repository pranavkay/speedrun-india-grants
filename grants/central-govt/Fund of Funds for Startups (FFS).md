# Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)

## Overview
FFS catalyzes venture capital investment into Indian startups. Operationalized by [[SIDBI]], it provides capital to SEBI-registered Alternative Investment Funds (AIFs), which in turn invest in startups. FFS 2.0 was approved by Cabinet to continue and expand the program.

As of October 2025, supported AIFs invested ~₹2,838.9 crore in **154 women-led startups**.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed through growth
- **Sectors**: All sectors
- **Entity Type**: Indian startups (applied via participating AIFs)
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Mechanism | SIDBI → SEBI AIFs → Startups |
| Corpus | FFS 2.0 expanded allocation |
| Equity | Yes (via AIF investment) |
| Duration | Fund life of participating AIFs |

## How to Apply
1. Startups apply to SIDBI-backed AIFs (not directly to SIDBI)
2. AIF evaluates and invests
3. List of participating AIFs on [SIDBI VCF portal](https://sidbivcf.in/)
