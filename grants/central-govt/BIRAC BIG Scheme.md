# BIRAC BIG (Biotechnology Ignition Grant)

## Overview
The Biotechnology Ignition Grant by [[BIRAC]] (Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council) provides up to **₹50 lakh** to support early-stage biotech startups and entrepreneurs with promising ideas. Funded by the Department of Biotechnology (DBT).

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Ideation to pre-seed
- **Sectors**: Biotechnology, healthcare, agriculture, industrial biotech
- **Entity Type**: Startups, individual entrepreneurs, scientists
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Amount | Up to ₹50 Lakh |
| Type | Non-refundable grant |
| Equity | None |
| Duration | 18 months typically |
