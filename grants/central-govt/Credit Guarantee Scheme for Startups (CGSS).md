# Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)

## Overview
CGSS provides credit guarantees to banks and financial institutions lending to DPIIT-recognized startups, enabling **collateral-free loans**. Part of the three flagship schemes under the Startup India initiative alongside [[Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)|SISFS]] and [[Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)|FFS]].

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to growth
- **Sectors**: All sectors
- **Entity Type**: DPIIT-recognized startups
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Type | Credit guarantee (collateral-free loans) |
| Equity | None |
| Channel | Banks and scheduled financial institutions |
