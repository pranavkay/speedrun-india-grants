# Atal Innovation Mission (AIC Grants)

## Overview
[[NITI Aayog]]'s flagship innovation program. Each Atal Incubation Centre (AIC) receives up to **₹10 crore over 5 years**. As of 2025, **72 AICs** are operational across India, having supported **3,500+ startups** and created **32,000+ jobs**.

Community Innovation Centres (ACICs) receive up to **₹2.5 crore** with matching partner funding.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Ideation to seed
- **Sectors**: All sectors
- **Entity Type**: Startups incubated at AICs
- **Geography**: 18 states, 3 UTs

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| AIC Grant | Up to ₹10 Crore over 5 years |
| ACIC Grant | Up to ₹2.5 Crore (50% matching) |
| Startups Supported | 3,500+ |
| Jobs Created | 32,000+ |
