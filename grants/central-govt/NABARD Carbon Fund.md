# NABARD Carbon Fund (NCF)

## Overview
Launched January 2026 alongside the [[NABARD Green Impact Fund]], with a **₹300 crore** corpus. Focuses on **carbon developers and startups** working on emissions reduction and carbon capture — sectors that are capital-intensive and largely underserved by conventional finance.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to early
- **Sectors**: Carbon capture, emissions reduction, carbon markets
- **Entity Type**: Carbon developers, climate startups
- **Geography**: Pan-India
