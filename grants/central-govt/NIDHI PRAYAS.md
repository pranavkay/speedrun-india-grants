# NIDHI PRAYAS

## Overview
NIDHI-PRAYAS (Promoting and Accelerating Young and Aspiring Innovators & Startups) is [[DST]]'s pre-incubation initiative providing up to **₹10 lakh** to translate innovative ideas into prototypes. Managed by SINE, IIT Bombay as the Program Management Unit (PMU), with PRAYAS Centres across India.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Ideation / pre-prototype
- **Sectors**: All sectors (tech emphasis)
- **Entity Type**: Individual innovators, aspiring entrepreneurs
- **Geography**: Pan-India, through PRAYAS Centres

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Amount | Up to ₹10 Lakh |
| Type | Non-refundable grant |
| Equity | None |
| PMU | SINE, IIT Bombay |
| Centres | Multiple PRAYAS Centres across India |

## How to Apply
1. Visit [NIDHI PRAYAS portal](https://nidhi-prayas.org/)
2. Identify nearest PRAYAS Centre
3. Submit application through the Centre
4. Centre evaluates and recommends
