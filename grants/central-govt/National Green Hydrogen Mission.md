# National Green Hydrogen Mission

## Overview
India's mission to become a global hub for green hydrogen production. Allocated incentives for **450,000 tonnes per annum** of green hydrogen production. Removal of import duty on lithium-ion cells for EV and BESS manufacturers complements this mission.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Early to growth (production capability)
- **Sectors**: Green hydrogen, electrolyzers, fuel cells, clean energy
- **Entity Type**: Companies, startups with production plans
- **Geography**: Pan-India
