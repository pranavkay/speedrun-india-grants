# ASPIRE Scheme (MSME)

## Overview
A Scheme for Promotion of Innovation, Rural Industries, and Entrepreneurship. Managed by [[SIDBI]] with a corpus of **₹310 crore**. Provides grants for setting up incubation and technology centres, with a focus on agro-industry and rural entrepreneurship.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Pre-seed to early
- **Sectors**: Agritech, rural industries, manufacturing, MSMEs
- **Entity Type**: MSMEs, startups, incubation centres
- **Geography**: Pan-India (rural emphasis)

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Government Grant | 100% of cost up to ₹1 Crore |
| PPP Mode | 50% of cost up to ₹50 Lakh |
| Corpus | ₹310 Crore (ASPIRE Fund) |
| Equity | None |
