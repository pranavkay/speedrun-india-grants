# ICAR Agri-Business Incubators

## Overview
[[ICAR]] operates **50 Agri-Business Incubators (ABIs)** across its research institutes. Has supported **818 startups** through the National Agriculture Innovation Fund (NAIF), launched in 2016-17. Notable incubator: a-IDEA at NAARM, Hyderabad (partnered with [[NABARD]]).

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Ideation to seed
- **Sectors**: Agriculture, food technology, agritech
- **Entity Type**: Agri-entrepreneurs, agritech startups
- **Geography**: Pan-India (50 institutes)
