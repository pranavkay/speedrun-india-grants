# IndiaAI Startup Financing

## Overview
The Startup Financing pillar of the [[IndiaAI Mission]] aims to democratize funding for AI entrepreneurs with a total allocation of **₹1,942.5 crore**. Includes the IndiaAI Startups Global Acceleration Programme (partnership with Station F and HEC Paris).

The 2026-27 Union Budget allocated an additional **₹10,372 crore** to the broader IndiaAI Mission.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to early growth AI startups
- **Sectors**: Artificial Intelligence
- **Entity Type**: DPIIT-registered AI startups
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Total Allocation | ₹1,942.5 Crore |
| Type | Grants, acceleration support |
| Equity | None |
| Global Track | 10 startups/cohort sent to Station F, Paris |

## How to Apply
1. Visit [IndiaAI Portal](https://indiaai.gov.in/hub/indiaai-startup-financing)
2. Register as a DPIIT-recognized startup
3. Apply to specific tracks (financing, acceleration, compute)
