# NABARD Green Impact Fund (NGIF)

## Overview
Launched in January 2026 by [[NABARD]] with a **₹1,000 crore** corpus. Uses interest income to lower borrowing costs for climate adaptation projects — crop diversification, water-efficient farming, green tech deployment. Targets the intersection of agriculture and climate technology.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to growth
- **Sectors**: Climate tech, clean energy, sustainable agriculture
- **Entity Type**: Startups, state governments, climate projects
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Mechanism | Interest income subsidizes borrowing |
| Equity | None |
