# BIRAC RDI Fund

## Overview
The RDI (Research, Development & Innovation) Fund by [[BIRAC]] offers substantial funding of **₹5 Crore to ₹200 Crore** for biotech, healthcare, deep tech, indigenous manufacturing, and bioengineering ventures. One of the largest non-dilutive grants available in India.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to growth (product/tech validated)
- **Sectors**: Biotech, healthcare, deep tech, manufacturing, bioengineering
- **Entity Type**: Indian companies, startups
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Amount | ₹5 Crore to ₹200 Crore |
| Type | Grant |

| Duration | Project-based |
