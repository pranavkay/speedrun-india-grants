# IndiaAI Compute Credits

## Overview
The compute capacity pillar of [[IndiaAI Mission]] provides access to **38,000+ GPUs** (with 20,000 more in pipeline) at heavily subsidized rates. One of the cheapest AI compute options globally at **₹65/hour**. NVIDIA is a key partner, with H100 SXM GPUs available.

For foundational AI model builders, **100% compute subsidy** is available — Sarvam AI received ₹98.68 crore in subsidies for 4,096 H100 GPUs.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: All stages
- **Sectors**: AI/ML
- **Entity Type**: DPIIT-registered startups, researchers, MSMEs, academic faculty, govt entities
- **Geography**: Pan-India

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| GPUs Available | 38,000+ (20,000 more planned) |
| Rate | ₹65/hr (up to 40% below market) |
| Foundational Models | 100% subsidy |
| Hardware | NVIDIA H100 SXM and others |

## How to Apply
1. Register on [IndiaAI Compute Portal](https://indiaai.gov.in/hub/indiaai-compute-capacity)
2. Verify eligibility (DPIIT registration, etc.)
3. Request compute allocation
