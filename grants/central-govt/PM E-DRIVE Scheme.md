# PM E-DRIVE Scheme

## Overview
With a **₹4,391 crore outlay**, PM E-DRIVE supports electric vehicle adoption and infrastructure. Has supported **14,028 electric buses** so far. Relevant for startups in EV manufacturing, charging infrastructure, and mobility tech.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Early to growth
- **Sectors**: Electric vehicles, charging infra, mobility tech
- **Entity Type**: EV manufacturers, fleet operators, infra providers
- **Geography**: Pan-India
