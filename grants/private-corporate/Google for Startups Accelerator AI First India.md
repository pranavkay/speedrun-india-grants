# Google for Startups Accelerator: AI First (India)

## Overview
Dedicated **AI-first track** of Google's India accelerator. Targets startups building with AI as a core differentiator. **Equity-free** with early access to Google AI products and Cloud TPU access.

## Benefits
- AI-specific mentorship from Google AI researchers
- Early access to Google AI products
- Cloud TPU access for ML research
- Product and business development workshops
