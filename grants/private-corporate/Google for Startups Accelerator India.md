# Google for Startups Accelerator India

## Overview
**Equity-free** accelerator running June–September 2026 for Seed to Series A startups in India. Includes expert pairing from Google and industry, workshops on product design, customer acquisition, and leadership.

## Benefits
- Equity-free participation
- Dedicated Google expert mentorship
- Cloud credits (subject to eligibility)
- Free Cloud TPU access
- Product design & growth workshops

## Application
- **Deadline**: April 19, 2026
- **Stage**: Seed to Series A
- [Apply here](https://startup.google.com/programs/accelerator/india/)
