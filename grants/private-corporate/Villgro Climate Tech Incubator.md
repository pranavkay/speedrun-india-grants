# Villgro Climate Tech Incubator

## Overview
Partnership between [[Villgro]] and Mercedes-Benz R&D India. Supports social enterprises in **Climate Action and Sustainable Mobility**. Critically, this program **takes no equity** — funding is provided solely on a **grant basis**.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Pre-seed to seed
- **Sectors**: Climate action, sustainable mobility
- **Entity Type**: Social enterprises, climate startups
- **Geography**: India
