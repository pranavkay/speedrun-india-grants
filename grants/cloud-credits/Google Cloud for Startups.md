# Google Cloud for Startups

## Overview
Google Cloud offers up to **$350,000 over two years** for AI-first startups through the **Scale Tier AI track**. Year 1 covers 100% of costs up to $250K; Year 2 covers 20% up to $100K. Combined credits across tiers can exceed **$500K**.

## Tiers
- **Build Tier**: Up to $2,000
- **Scale Tier**: Up to $200,000 over 2 years
- **Scale Tier AI**: Up to $350,000 over 2 years
