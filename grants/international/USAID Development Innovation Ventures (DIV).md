# USAID Development Innovation Ventures (DIV)

## Overview
DIV provides **tiered, flexible grant funding** to innovations across 46 countries. Total of **$106M** disbursed to **190+ innovations**. Three stages: pilot → test → scale.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Ideation (Stage 1) through scale-up (Stage 3)
- **Sectors**: Agriculture, health, climate, education, economic growth
- **Entity Type**: For-profit, non-profit, social enterprises globally
- **Geography**: Low and Middle Income Countries (India eligible)

## Funding Details

| Parameter | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| Stage 1 (Pilot) | Up to $200K |
| Stage 2 (Test) | Up to $1.5M |
| Stage 3 (Scale) | Up to $15M |
| Total Disbursed | $106M to 190+ innovations |
| Equity | None |
