# EU EIC Accelerator 2026

## Overview
One of Europe's most competitive funding programmes for breakthrough innovations. Provides up to **€2.5M in non-dilutive grants** plus optional equity investment up to **€15M**. The 2026 budget is **€220M for Challenges** and **€414M for Open track**.

Six cut-off dates per year in 2026.

## Eligibility for Indian Startups
- EU grants primarily require entities registered in EU member states or Horizon Europe associated countries
- Indian founders can be eligible if they **incorporate in an EU country**
- Some tracks open to associated countries (Norway, Switzerland, Israel, etc.)
- EU-India Joint Strategic Agenda (adopted Jan 2026) may expand future access

## How to Apply
1. Visit [EIC Accelerator portal](https://eic.ec.europa.eu/eic-funding-opportunities/eic-accelerator_en)
2. Submit short application during open cut-off
3. If shortlisted, submit full application
4. Pitch to EIC jury
