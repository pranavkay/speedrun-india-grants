# IndiaAI Global Acceleration (Station F)

## Overview
Part of [[IndiaAI Startup Financing]], this programme sends **10 Indian AI startups per cohort** to **Station F (Paris)** and **HEC Paris** for European market access, mentorship, and investor networking.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to Series A AI startups
- **Sectors**: AI/ML
- **Entity Type**: DPIIT-registered Indian AI startups
- **Geography**: India-based, expanding to EU

## Benefits
- Residency at Station F, Paris
- HEC Paris business mentorship
- European investor access
- Market expansion support
