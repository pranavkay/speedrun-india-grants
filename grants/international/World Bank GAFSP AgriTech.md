# World Bank GAFSP AgriTech Grants

## Overview
The Global Agriculture and Food Security Program (GAFSP) and IFC provide funding and technical assistance to AgriTech startups in Low and Middle Income Countries. In FY2025, IFC committed a record **$71.7 billion** to private companies in developing countries.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to early
- **Sectors**: AgriTech, food security, agriculture productivity
- **Entity Type**: Startups and businesses in LMICs
- **Geography**: India eligible as LMIC
