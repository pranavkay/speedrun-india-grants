# EU-India Joint Research Cooperation

## Overview
Part of the **Joint EU-India Comprehensive Strategic Agenda Towards 2030**, adopted January 27, 2026 at the 16th EU-India Summit. Includes sponsored visits, joint R&D, and investment facilitation for startups. Notable focus on **EV battery recycling** collaboration.

## Current Tracks
- EV battery recycling (matchmaking led to joint plant plans)
- Clean energy research
- Digital/AI cooperation
