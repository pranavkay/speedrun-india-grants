# Karnataka Deep-Tech Elevate

## Overview
Commenced **January 1, 2026**, this targeted deep-tech track selects **40 startups per cohort** to receive grants of **₹50 lakh to ₹1 crore** in AI, semiconductors, quantum technologies, robotics, and advanced materials. No equity taken.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Pre-seed to seed
- **Sectors**: AI, semiconductors, quantum tech, robotics, advanced materials
- **Entity Type**: Deep-tech startups in Karnataka
- **Geography**: Karnataka

## Funding Details
