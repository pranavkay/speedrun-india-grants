# Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) Grants

## Overview
Kerala Startup Mission provides incubation centres, grants, and international networking services to technology, healthcare, and education startups. Also hosts [[NIDHI PRAYAS]] centres. Prioritizes **women-led ventures** and deep-tech innovation.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Ideation to seed
- **Sectors**: Technology, healthcare, education
- **Entity Type**: Kerala-based startups
- **Geography**: Kerala

## Related Grants

- [[Tamil Nadu TANSEED 6.0]] — neighboring state alternative
