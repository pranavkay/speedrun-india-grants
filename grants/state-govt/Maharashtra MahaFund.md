# Maharashtra MahaFund

## Overview
A **₹500 crore** fund to support Maharashtra's startup ecosystem. Operates as a fund-of-funds model, investing through AIFs into startups.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to growth
- **Sectors**: All sectors
- **Entity Type**: Maharashtra-based startups
- **Geography**: Maharashtra (Mumbai, Pune focus)
