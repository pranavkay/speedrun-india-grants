# Telangana T-Hub Programs

## Overview
T-Hub is one of India's largest technology incubators, backed by the Government of Telangana. Offers multiple acceleration and incubation programs. Telangana's startup policy prioritizes **women-led ventures** and **deep-tech innovation**.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Pre-seed to early
- **Sectors**: Tech, deep tech, AI
- **Entity Type**: Startups (Telangana presence preferred)
- **Geography**: Telangana / Hyderabad
