# Maharashtra Startup Week Work Orders

## Overview
Winners receive **government work orders up to ₹15 Lakh** to pilot their solution with state departments. Unique model providing startups with a government customer, not just funding. Also offers IPR subsidy: up to ₹2L for domestic patents, ₹10L for international patents.

Maharashtra aims to support **50,000 startups by 2030**, with focus on Mumbai (Fintech/Media) and Pune (Auto/Engineering).

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Seed to early (working product)
- **Sectors**: All, with govtech emphasis
- **Entity Type**: Maharashtra-registered startups
- **Geography**: Maharashtra
