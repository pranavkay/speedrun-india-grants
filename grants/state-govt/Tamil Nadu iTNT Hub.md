# Tamil Nadu iTNT Hub

## Overview
Focuses exclusively on **Deep Tech, AI, and IoT**. Facilitates grants up to **₹50 Lakh** for R&D-heavy startups. Part of Tamil Nadu's emerging technology ecosystem.

## Eligibility
- **Stage**: Pre-seed to seed
- **Sectors**: Deep Tech, AI, IoT exclusively
- **Entity Type**: R&D-focused startups
- **Geography**: Tamil Nadu
