# Grants by Company Stage

## Ideation / Pre-Prototype
> For innovators with an idea but no product yet

- [[NIDHI PRAYAS]] — up to ₹10 lakh for prototype development
- [[Atal Innovation Mission (AIC Grants)]] — access to incubation infrastructure
- [[MeitY GENESIS]] — up to ₹10 lakh for early-stage tech startups
- [[NABARD National Climate Stack Challenge]] — prize money for climate ideas

## Pre-Seed / Seed
> DPIIT-registered startups, early revenue or pre-revenue

- [[Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)]] — up to ₹20L grant + ₹50L debt
- [[Karnataka Elevate Programme]] — up to ₹50 lakh equity-free
- [[Karnataka Deep-Tech Elevate]] — ₹50L to ₹1Cr for deep tech
- [[Tamil Nadu TANSEED 6.0]] — ₹10 lakh seed grant
- [[Tamil Nadu iTNT Hub]] — up to ₹50 lakh for R&D
- [[Maharashtra Startup Week Work Orders]] — govt work orders up to ₹15L
- [[Kerala Startup Mission (KSUM) Grants]]
- [[BIRAC BIG Scheme]] — up to ₹50 lakh for biotech
- [[Villgro Climate Tech Incubator]] — grant-based, no equity
- [[Villgro Impact Incubation]] — seed funding (debt/equity/grant)
- [[Google for Startups Accelerator India]] — equity-free, credits + mentorship
- [[Google for Startups Accelerator AI First India]] — equity-free, AI-specific
- [[Microsoft for Startups Founders Hub]] — credits + enterprise access
- [[NVIDIA Inception Program]] — GPU credits, co-marketing
- [[AWS Activate]] — up to $100K cloud credits
- [[Google Cloud for Startups]] — up to $200K–$350K credits
- [[Microsoft Azure for Startups]] — up to $150K credits

- [[MeitY AI Innovation Programme]] — up to ₹10 crore for AI
- [[IndiaAI Startup Financing]] — ₹1,942.5 crore allocated
- [[IndiaAI Compute Credits]] — GPUs at ₹65/hr (40% subsidy)
- [[BIRAC RDI Fund]] — ₹5Cr to ₹200Cr for deep tech
- [[BIRAC Product Commercialization Program]] — scale-up support for 24 months
- [[ASPIRE Scheme (MSME)]] — up to ₹1Cr for plant & machinery
- [[EU EIC Accelerator 2026]] — up to €2.5M grant + €15M equity
- [[USAID Development Innovation Ventures (DIV)]] — tiered grant funding
- [[IndiaAI Global Acceleration (Station F)]] — EU market access
- [[Maharashtra MahaFund]] — ₹500 crore corpus
