# Grants by Size

## Micro Grants (up to ₹10 Lakh / ~$12K)
- [[NIDHI PRAYAS]] — up to ₹10 lakh
- [[MeitY GENESIS]] — up to ₹10 lakh
- [[Tamil Nadu TANSEED 6.0]] — ₹10 lakh

- [[BIRAC BIG Scheme]] — up to ₹50 lakh

- [[Maharashtra Startup Week Work Orders]] — up to ₹15 lakh

- [[ASPIRE Scheme (MSME)]] — up to ₹1 crore
- [[AWS Activate]] — up to $100K credits
- [[Microsoft Azure for Startups]] — up to $150K credits

## Medium Grants (₹1 Crore – ₹10 Crore / $120K–$1.2M)
- [[Karnataka Deep-Tech Elevate]] — ₹50L to ₹1 crore
- [[MeitY AI Innovation Programme]] — up to ₹10 crore
- [[EU EIC Accelerator 2026]] — up to €2.5M (~₹23 crore) grant component
- [[Google Cloud for Startups]] — up to $350K credits (~₹3 crore)

- [[IndiaAI Compute Credits]] — 38,000+ GPUs, 100% subsidy for foundational models
- [[BIRAC RDI Fund]] — ₹5 crore to ₹200 crore
- [[Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)]] — invests via AIFs
- [[NABARD Green Impact Fund]] — ₹1,000 crore corpus
- [[NABARD Carbon Fund]] — ₹300 crore corpus
