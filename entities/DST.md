# DST

**Department of Science and Technology**, under the Ministry of Science and Technology. Runs the NIDHI (National Initiative for Developing and Harnessing Innovations) umbrella program.

## Schemes Administered
- [[NIDHI PRAYAS]] — pre-incubation grants up to ₹10L
- NIDHI-SSS (Seed Support System)
- NIDHI-EIR (Entrepreneur in Residence)
- Technology Development Board (TDB) grants

## Key Role
- Funds prototype development and early innovation
- SINE, IIT Bombay is the Program Management Unit for PRAYAS
