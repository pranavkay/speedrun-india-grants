# NABARD

**National Bank for Agriculture and Rural Development**. Launched major climate and agritech funds in 2026.

## Schemes Administered
- [[NABARD Green Impact Fund]] — ₹1,000 crore climate fund (Jan 2026)
- [[NABARD Carbon Fund]] — ₹300 crore carbon fund (Jan 2026)
- [[NABARD National Climate Stack Challenge]] — with Gates Foundation
- AgriSURE fund for agritech startups
