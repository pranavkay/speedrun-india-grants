# SIDBI

**Small Industries Development Bank of India**. Operationalizes the Fund of Funds for Startups and manages ASPIRE Fund.

## Schemes Managed
- [[Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)]] — invests in SEBI AIFs
- [[ASPIRE Scheme (MSME)]] — ₹310 crore fund for rural/MSME innovation

## Key Role
- India's principal development financial institution for MSMEs
- Channel for government venture capital into the startup ecosystem
