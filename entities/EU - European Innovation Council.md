# EU - European Innovation Council

The EIC is the EU's flagship innovation agency under Horizon Europe.

## Programs Relevant to India
- [[EU EIC Accelerator 2026]] — up to €2.5M grant + €15M equity
- [[EU-India Joint Research Cooperation]] — bilateral R&D
- [[IndiaAI Global Acceleration (Station F)]] — India-to-EU pipeline

## Note on Eligibility
Indian startups typically need to incorporate in an EU member state to access EIC grants directly. The EU-India Strategic Agenda (Jan 2026) may expand future access.
