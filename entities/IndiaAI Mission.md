# IndiaAI Mission

India's national AI mission under [[MeitY]]. The 2026-27 Union Budget allocated **₹10,372 crore** to the mission.

## Pillars
- [[IndiaAI Startup Financing]] — ₹1,942.5 crore for AI startups
- [[IndiaAI Compute Credits]] — 38,000+ GPUs at subsidized rates
- [[IndiaAI Global Acceleration (Station F)]] — EU market access
- IndiaAI Datasets Platform
- IndiaAI FutureSkills (training)

## Key Partners
- [[NVIDIA]] — 20,000+ GPUs deployed
- Station F, HEC Paris — global acceleration
