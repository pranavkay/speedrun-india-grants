# BIRAC

**Biotechnology Industry Research Assistance Council**, a not-for-profit under the Department of Biotechnology (DBT). India's primary agency for biotech and life science startup funding.

## Schemes Administered
- [[BIRAC BIG Scheme]] — up to ₹50L ignition grants
- [[BIRAC RDI Fund]] — ₹5Cr to ₹200Cr for R&D
- [[BIRAC Product Commercialization Program]] — scale-up support
- [[DBT-BIRAC BioAI Call]] — AI + biomanufacturing
- BioNEST (bioincubator program)

## Key Role
- Largest biotech-specific grant agency in India
- Funds from ideation through commercialization
