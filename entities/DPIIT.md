# DPIIT

**Department for Promotion of Industry and Internal Trade**, under the Ministry of Commerce and Industry. Administers the **Startup India** initiative and DPIIT startup recognition.

As of October 2025, DPIIT has officially recognized **1,97,692 startups**.

## Schemes Administered
- [[Startup India Seed Fund Scheme (SISFS)]]
- [[Fund of Funds for Startups (FFS)]]
- [[Credit Guarantee Scheme for Startups (CGSS)]]

## Key Role
- DPIIT recognition is a prerequisite for most central govt startup schemes
- Register at [Startup India Portal](https://www.startupindia.gov.in/)
